## Summary

This neighborhood in Portland's Nob Hill area of the Northwest District enjoys exceptional walkability and public transit access. The area is a **Walker's Paradise** — daily errands do not require a car. 89 restaurants, 6 healthcare facilities, and 8 transit stops are all within a 1km walk. The Portland Streetcar (NS Line, A Loop, B Loop) serves the area with the nearest stop just 128m away (2-minute walk).
